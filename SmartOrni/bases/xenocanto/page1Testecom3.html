 'Inambari-Tambopata' Antwren (Herpsilochmus sp.nov.Inambari_Tambopata) 0:45 David Geale 2011-05-27 10:10 Peru Pajchani Track, Puno 1540 Call

River noise & a car horn. After playback of H. motacilloides. At least 4 birds present, apparently a male vocalising. 7-9 m. above ground in canopy of remnant patch of montane forest surrounded by mostly citrus plantations. No mixed flock. This taxon looks very much like H. parkeri (both sexes), but sounds more like H. motacilloides.
